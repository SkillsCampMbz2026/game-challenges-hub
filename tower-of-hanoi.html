How to play

  • Click a tower to pick up its top disk, then click another tower to drop it.
  • Or drag the top disk from one tower onto another.
  • A disk can never rest on a smaller disk.
  • Only the top disk of a tower can move (it's outlined).
  • Win by rebuilding the full stack on the Goal tower.

The minimum number of moves is 2ⁿ − 1 for n disks. Match it for a ✨ perfect run.
