How to play

  • Click any cell to toggle it and its four orthogonal neighbours (up, down, left, right).
  • Lit cells glow; your goal is to turn every cell off.
  • Every starting board is randomised but always solvable.
  • Stuck? Press 💡 Hint to reveal one move from an optimal solution.
  • Match the Minimum move count for a ✨ perfect run.

Harder difficulties start from boards whose shortest solution needs more moves.
