How to play

  • ▲ / ↑ / W — step forward one cell.
  • ▼ / ↓ / S — step backward one cell.
  • ⟲ / ← / A and ⟳ / → / D — turn 90°.
  • The minimap (top-right) reveals where you've been.
  • Head for the gold walls — that's the exit.
  • Every maze is randomly generated and always solvable.
  • Match the Shortest step count for a ✨ perfect escape.

Only forward/back steps count as moves — turning is free. Press 💡 Show path to briefly reveal the shortest route on the minimap.
